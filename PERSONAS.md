## Notes

These personas are **composite archetypes** built from actual quiz response patterns and represent the diversity of users coming to this platform:

- **Tech professionals** range from junior/exploring to senior specialists looking for next steps
- **Non-tech switchers** span career transitioners with prior experience and complete fresh graduates
- **Experience levels** cover 0-8+ years of professional work
- **Motivations** include ambition, lifestyle, interest, stability, and compensation
- **Learning approaches** vary from structured bootcamps to self-directed learning to on-the-job growth

Each persona maps directly to the quiz fields shown in `QUIZ_QUESTIONS_AND_OPTIONS.md`.
